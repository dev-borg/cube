
§ cgroups (aka. control groups)

- cgroups aka. control groups

- linux kernel feature 

- allows for allocation of system resources among processes

- function:
	° provide a way to organize processes into hierarchical groups
	° apply resource limits (CPU, disk usage, network bandwidth)
	° prioritize access to resources
	° isolate resource usage

- usefulness
	° environments where important to manageme and control resource allocation
	° between different processes or groups of processes

- usage
	° containers, cf. Docker and Kubernetes
